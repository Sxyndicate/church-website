Join the Team

Thank you for expressing interest in volunteering with COG - Church of God! Your decision to step into a volunteer role is a powerful contribution to our community.

At COG, volunteering is not just a task; it's an invitation to be part of a family committed to making a positive impact. As a volunteer, you become an essential part of the heartbeat of our church, where your unique skills and passions find purpose.


Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Ushering & Security.

Join the frontlines of hospitality and safety as part of our Ushering & Security Ministry. As a vital part of our worship experience, ushers create a warm and welcoming environment for every attendee. Your role involves assisting with seating, providing information, and ensuring a secure atmosphere

Kids Church.

Step into the joy-filled world of our Kids Church Ministry, where little hearts encounter the love of God in exciting and age-appropriate ways. As a volunteer, you become a guide, mentor, and source of inspiration for these young minds, helping them build a foundation of faith that lasts a lifetime.

Music Team.

Join the harmonious heartbeat of our worship experience by becoming a part of the Music Team Ministry. As a musician or vocalist, you'll play a crucial role in creating an atmosphere of praise and adoration, guiding the congregation into a deeper connection with God through the power of music.

Prayer Team.

Step into the transformative realm of intercession by joining the Prayer Team Ministry. As a prayer warrior, you'll play a crucial role in covering our church family, community, and beyond in prayer. This ministry is a sacred space where hearts connect with God, seeking His guidance, healing, and intervention.

Administrative Support.

Become the behind-the-scenes hero of COG - Church of God by joining the Administrative Support Ministry. As a volunteer in this essential role, you'll contribute to the smooth functioning of our church operations, ensuring that every administrative detail is handled with precision and care.

Production Design.

Immerse yourself in the creative world of worship by joining the Production Design Ministry. As a volunteer, you'll contribute to crafting visually engaging and spiritually enriching experiences during our services and events. From lighting and visuals to stage design, your creativity will play a pivotal role in enhancing the worship atmosphere.

Technical & Stage Management.

Step into the dynamic realm of technical excellence by joining the Technical & Stage Management Ministry. As a key contributor to our worship experience, you'll play a crucial role in managing audio, video, and stage elements to ensure seamless and impactful services.

Communications.

Embark on a journey of effective storytelling and connection by joining the Communications Ministry at COG - Church of God. As a communications volunteer, you'll play a vital role in conveying the heart and soul of our church to both the congregation and the broader community through various channels.
